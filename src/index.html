Lionel Messi

The Worlds Greatest Player

  • 1987: Lionel Messi was born on June 24,1987 , in Rosario, Argentina.
  • 1995: Messi joined to play for the local team, Newell's Old boys in Rosario.
  • 1998: At the age of 11, he was diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency.
  • 2000: Messi signs with legendary team FC Barcelona and works his way up through Barca's youth squads.
  • 2003: Messi made his unofficial debut for FC Barcelona in a friendly match against FC Porto.
  • 2004: Messi made his official debut against RCD Espanyol
  • 2005: At the age of 18, Messi scored his first official goal and was the youngest player to score for FC Barcelona.
  • 2007: Messi establishes 'Leo Messi Foundation', working to improve access education and health care for children.
  • 2009: Messi wins FIFA World player of the year.
  • 2010: Messi wins his first Ballon D'or ( Golden Ball award ).
  • 2011: Messi wins his second Ballon D'or ( Golden Ball award).
  • 2012: Messi wins his third Ballon D'or ( Golden Ball award ).
  • 2011-2012: Messi sets the all-time record for the most goals scored in a single season for a major European football league, with 73 goals.
  • 2013: Messi wins his fourth consecutive record- breaking Ballon D'or ( Golden Ball award ).
  • 2014: Messi scores a hat-trick ( three goals during a single game ), to become FC Barcelona's all-time leading scorer with 371 goals for Barca, beating the record set by Paulino Alcantara, who scored 369 goals.
  • 2014: Messi wins the Golden Ball award for the best player in the 2014 FIFA World Cup.
  • 2014: Messi becomes the leading goalscorer in the European Champions League with 74 goals in 91 appearances.

    • Roy Keane (Ex ManUnited) – “I was a big fan of Maradona growing up and of the current crop Ronaldo is good but Messi is the best I’ve ever seen. I don’t dish out praise lightly but Messi deserves it. I look for weaknesses in his game and I can’t find them.”
